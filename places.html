Must See Places

Amazing Every Tourist Needs To Visit!

Lapaz Sand Dunes

A trip to the La Paz Sand Dunes is an absolute must for those seeking an adrenaline rush. These sprawling dunes, stretching for miles, offer an unparalleled experience for thrill-seekers. From the towering sand hills to the vast expanse of the desert, the La Paz Sand Dunes provide an unbeatable backdrop for various exciting activities. Sandboarding, or riding a board down the dunes, is a popular pastime here; the wind-sculpted sand hills provide the perfect opportunity to test your skills. Off-road driving is also available for the more adventurous, allowing visitors to explore the sand dunes in their own vehicles. With its unique landscape and thrilling activities, the La Paz Sand Dunes make the perfect destination for adventure seekers.

Museo Ilocos Norte

Immerse yourself in the culture and heritage of Ilocano at the Museo Ilocos Norte! From ancient pottery and tools to intricate artwork and traditional costumes, the museum offers a unique glimpse into the history and culture of the province. Explore a diverse range of artifacts, artworks, and historical items that tell the story of Ilocos Norte and its people. Learn about the province’s unique cuisine, traditional music, and vibrant religious and spiritual practices. With its wide array of exhibits, the Museo Ilocos Norte provides an unforgettable experience that will leave you with a newfound appreciation for the province’s rich cultural heritage. Whether you are a history buff or simply looking for a unique cultural experience, the Museo Ilocos Norte will be a memorable visit.

Aurora Park

Located in the center of Laoag City, Aurora Park is a stunning public park full of lush greenery and vibrant flowers. The park’s centerpiece is a magnificent bronze statue of former Philippine President Manuel L. Quezon, standing tall and proud. The park is a favorite spot for locals and tourists, providing a tranquil atmosphere and a chance to appreciate the local culture. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll through the park, taking in the sights and sounds of the surrounding area. The park also features a variety of benches, tables, and gazebos, perfect for relaxing and enjoying a picnic lunch. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful place to unwind or an opportunity to learn more about the history of the Philippines, Aurora Park is a great destination.

Sinking Bell Tower

The majestic Laoag Sinking Bell Tower stands tall in the city, a reminder of its remarkable history. Built in the late 19th century with coral stones and red bricks, it stands at the height of over 30 feet, adorned with intricate designs and sculptures. Its bell still rings daily, can be heard from miles away, and is a source of pride for the locals. Despite its sandy foundation, the tower has stood for over a century, captivating visitors worldwide with its unique charm. This iconic landmark is a marvel of engineering and a testament to the city’s resilience, making it a must-see destination for anyone visiting Laoag City.
